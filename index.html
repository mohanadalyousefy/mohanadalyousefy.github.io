About me

I am a research associate at the WHO Collaborating Centre for Health Literacy (WHO-CCHL). I have completed a Master of Science in Health Prevention and a Bachelor of Dental Surgery.

My research focuses on oral oncology, HPV-related cancers, systematic review methodology, and adolescent health literacy. I have published five peer-reviewed papers with 81 citations and an H-index of 3.

I came to Germany on a competitive DAAD scholarship and graduated in the top 10% of my M.Sc. programme at TUM. I have built a research career across Yemen, Saudi Arabia, and Germany, working at the intersection of rigorous scientific analysis and clear communication.
